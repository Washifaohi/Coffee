About us

Hello! I'm Washifa Islam, a passionate web developer and the proud owner of Washifa's Coffee House. Located in the heart of our community, we strive to create a unique and inviting space where everyone feels welcome. Our coffee house is more than just a place to grab a drink; it's a hub for creativity, connection, and inspiration.

At Washifa's, we serve a carefully curated selection of high-quality coffees sourced from around the world. Whether you're in the mood for a rich espresso or a creamy cappuccino, our skilled baristas are here to craft the perfect cup for you. Don't forget to indulge in our delicious pastries and light snacks, all made with love from locally sourced ingredients.

We also host a variety of events, from art showcases to live music nights, bringing together talented local artists and fostering a sense of community. Whether you're looking for a cozy spot to work or a place to unwind with friends, Washifa's Coffee House is the perfect destination. Join us and experience the warmth and creativity that makes Washifa's a beloved gathering place. We can't wait to serve you!
